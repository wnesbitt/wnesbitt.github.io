How we met

I first met Allie about eight years ago when I asked her to prom. What began as simple date to prom quickly grew into something special as we started spending more time together. We decided to make things official the day before her birthday – I had no idea about the timing... but it did make anniversaries easy! We both went to the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, and that’s where our relationship really blossomed. From long study sessions to spontaneous adventures, we never missed a chance to be together. Finally, I popped the question, and here we are! :)
